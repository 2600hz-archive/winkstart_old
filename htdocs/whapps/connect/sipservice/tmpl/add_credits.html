Pay as you go credits will be used for international and per-minute billing.

Credits are separate from monthly recurring charges such as flat-rate circuits, DIDs and E911 charges.

Automatic Recharge:

Automatic Recharge allows you to Top Up your account balance automatically every time your account balance drops below $1.00. This way, your important calls will not be disconnected because of the insufficient credit and you don't need to log into your account in order to increase your credit manually.
